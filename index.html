About Me

I’m part of the Vulnerability, Accessibility, Inclusion & Bereavements team at Monzo, where I’m passionate about creating a more inclusive and accessible world. My work involves developing strategies that support vulnerable individuals and ensure everyone feels valued, especially during challenging times like bereavement.

I also volunteer as a Panel Member at Children's Hearings Scotland, where I’m dedicated to making a difference in the lives of young people, ensuring their voices are heard and respected.

Beyond work, I love going to concerts, diving into true crime stories, all things pugs, and exploring new foodie adventures. Let’s connect and collaborate!
